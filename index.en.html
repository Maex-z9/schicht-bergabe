The problem

What goes wrong at every shift change.

Lost notes, language barriers, tool chaos — the things that cost time, material and nerves.

  • Language barriers cost the most

    Polish, Turkish, Romanian, German — written in whatever works. Understood in whatever's left. Every handover turns into broken telephone.

  • Notes disappear

    Slips end up under the vice, in the locker, or in the bin. The next shift starts blind.

  • Tool chaos

    Who's got the torque wrench? Which machine is still running? Half the shift goes into looking.

  • Knowledge gets lost

    What we spotted yesterday, no one remembers today. The same issues come back — again and again. Documentation dies at clock-out.

The solution

A digital handover log that forgets nothing.

Six features that do exactly what the shop floor needs — and nothing more.

  • 01

    Automatic translation

    Write in your language, colleagues read in theirs. DE, EN, PL, TR, RO, RU and more.

  • 02

    Structured templates

    Machine, job, tools, open points. Defined fields instead of free-form prose.

  • 03

    Acknowledgement

    Whoever takes over the shift confirms each item. Nothing slips, responsibility is clear.

  • 04

    Full history

    Every handover stays searchable. Recurring problems become visible instead of happening again.

  • 05

    Tool overview

    Who has what, which machine is running, what's down — at a glance, instead of running through the hall.

  • 06

    On any device

    Phone, tablet, workshop PC. In the browser, no install, no IT department needed.
